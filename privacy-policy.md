# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

## 1. Who we are

LoFi Travel ("we", "us", "our") operates a content automation service that generates and publishes lo-fi travel videos.

Contact: hello@lofitravel.app

## 2. What data we process

We may process:
- TikTok account authorization data (OAuth tokens and related identifiers)
- Video metadata (captions, publish status, timestamps)
- Technical logs required for debugging and reliability

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## 3. Why we process data

We process data only to:
- authenticate your TikTok account,
- upload and publish content you request,
- monitor and improve service reliability.

## 4. Legal basis

Where applicable, we process data based on consent, contract performance, and legitimate interest in operating and securing the service.

## 5. Data sharing

We share data only with:
- TikTok APIs (to perform requested publishing actions),
- service providers strictly necessary for hosting, storage, and operations.

## 6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for service operation, security, and legal compliance.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data processing.

To make a request, contact: hello@lofitravel.app

## 8. Data deletion

You can request account and data deletion by emailing hello@lofitravel.app with the subject "Data Deletion Request".  
We will verify the request and delete eligible data within a reasonable period.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect processed data.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
